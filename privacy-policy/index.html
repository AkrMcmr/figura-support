Figura Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Figura is designed as a local-first private photo vault for personal body progress records.

Data Collection

Figura does not collect personal data.

The app does not operate a server account system, analytics pipeline, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party data sharing integration.

Photos and Records

Photos captured in Figura are not saved to the standard iOS Photos app. They are stored inside the app's local vault on your device.

Photo files are encrypted on device. Metadata used by the app, such as capture dates and selected body-part categories, is stored locally on device.

Authentication and Recovery

Figura uses device authentication, such as Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode, to protect access to the app.

The recovery passphrase is used to protect recovery information and encrypted backups. Figura cannot recover your passphrase for you.

Backups

Figura can export an encrypted backup file when you choose to do so. You control where that backup file is saved or shared.

Encrypted app data may also be included in device backups depending on your iOS backup settings.

Third Parties

Figura does not sell data and does not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Children

Figura is not directed to children.

Changes
